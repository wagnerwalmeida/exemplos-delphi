VCL Common Dialog Styling Demo for Screenshots

If you get a style error while compiling the project, make sure that all styles that are checked in Project > Options > Application > Appearance exist on your system.
To show premium styles in this demo, you can download the XE8 VCL premium style pack:
http://cc.embarcadero.com/item/30179

Extract the files, and place them into the Styles directory on your hard drive. You should then be able to enable them under  
Project > Options > Application > Appearance to use them in the app for screenshots etc.
